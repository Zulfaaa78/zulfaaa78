Features!

User Info

Showcases time played in seconds. Also has an achievement system that pops up when unlocked (See Slide 6)! Some achievements include making your first avatar, naming an avatar, creating all three avatars, creating some easter egg characters, and more.

Avatar Selection Menu!

The main menu where you can name your avatars (and edit them if you spell it incorrectly), check your user info, and most importantly, make your avatars!

Avatar Editing!

Where you edit your avatar! You can choose between three skin tones and pick the gender of your avatar! You can scroll through the clothes after and once satisfied, press done! All your avatars will be shown together at the end (Slide 8!).
